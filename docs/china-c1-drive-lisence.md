# China drive license (C1) test short guide

## Class Overview

Class One.Theory test: do some quiz on computer.

Class Two.Field test: include variant subject to perform low speed,
short distance and accurate maneuver,
test in a wide & safe dedicated test field.

Class Three.Road test:
drive in public road and do some action include overtake,turn,change gear...

Class Four.Safty knowladge test: do more quiz on computer.

### term list:
    1. gear null(gears not connect,no power deliver to wheel)
            1(0~10km/h)
            2(10~30km/h)
            3(25~45km/h)
            4(35~70km/h)
            5(60~120km/h)
            reverse(go backward)
    2. line: don't hit it, the lane boarders,limit where you can go.
    3. dash: can hit it, assist you to understand your current poisiton.
    4. back mirror: the glass mirrors let you able to monitor back left/right/middle.   
    5. hand-brake: let the veicle stop perminently.

    6. clutch: seperate the enginee and gear box, dynamicly adjust the output power.
    7. brake: slow the viecle by smash two metal peice together.
    8. throttle: let engine produce more power, speed up veicle.
    9. seat bolt: when crash happen, tie you to the place you should be.
    10. dashbard: your veicle information display here mostly.
    11. turning light: let out-side world understand where you want go.

## Class Two

Now,we need practice in real phycial world,
musle memory and other tricks kick in,
only practive more, review more gonna work.
Following guide is for pass the test,
you should learn your own pratical way to drive later
after get the drive-lisence.

### start

When you get in to a veicle, you should perform following steps:
    1. tie the seat belt.
    2. adjust the seat horizontal position to when you tread the clutch pannel
       to the deepest positon, your leg will bend to about 30 degree inner angle.
    3. adjust the seat vertical positon to when you put your hand
       between head and ceiling, it won't be stuck.
    4. adjust back mirrors to proper position.
        4.1 left mirror should let you able to peek the full wheel border.
        4.2 right mirror should put the door handle in the middle
            and little bit left.
    5. turn the key in to the viecle self-test positon,
       check all dashboard light is normal,
       check hand-brake status, check ceiling light is off.
    6. (!!)tread the clutch pannel in to deepest position.
    7. turn the key in to the start mode in short period
       (~2 sec,more in cold condition), and back,
       engine should start.

### move it

If you want to move forward, do this:
    1. (!!)tread the clutch pannel in to depest position.
    2. change gear to 1.
    3. up your clutch pannel in to semi-connect status
       (little vibrate from the clutch).
    4. hold the clutch pannel, match the clutch panel postion and
       the vibrate amplitude to have a fuzzy sense of where to enter
       semi-connect status, and where is it limit(engine stop).
    5. if enginee stop, change gear to none,
       and perform start engine instruction 6-7,
       and start this section from start. 
    5. drop hand-brake.

### stop it

If you don't want to fall from cliff, do this:
    1. tread brake pannel to active brakes.
    2. when the speed is slower then gear minimum speed limit,
       tread the clutch pannel to the end.
    3. if you want to leave the veicle, rise hand-brake.

### where to go

Slowly move forward,regain yourself to standard positon,
align the center of veicle to the left line of road,
same time, align the middle rain brush rivet( at a little bit left)
to the center of two wheel trace,
or simply imaging the wheel positon of the veicle and
align your wheel with wheel trace on road,
this techinique is usefull though the full test.

(!!) caution, when you get it to your veicle,
do "start" imediatly!
(!!) caution, if next subject is veicle in it,
stop and wait before the system think the subject is started,
also should not stop if previous subject is not finish.


### Subject One - start on ramp

This subject require great control to the clutch,
easy to fail, need practive more!
If engine stop at the middle of road,
tread brake pannel to stop the veicle and redo step 7 
    1. perform "where to go"
    2. when you just about to hit the ramp,
       up your clutch pannel for more power,
       at least 2/3 of the clutch pannel.
    3. adjust the clutch pannel with small velocity,
       it should be take care like a baby,
       at the same time, stable your positon on road,
       just perform "where to go"
    4. when the veicle front close the stop line,
       slow down more, also be as gentle as possiable,
       it is really easy to fail.
    5. when the left back mirror botton boarder hit the uppest line,
       tread the brake pannel to the half,
       and tread the clutch pannel a little bit deeper.
    6. rise the hand-brake and drop it after 5 second.
    7. up the clutch pannel a little bit upper,
       slowly release the brake pannel,
       and turn the wheel a little bit left.
    8. when you hit the top of ramp, use brake to control speed,
       let the earth drag you down.
       Don't forget to turn left,
       or you will touch the right line at the start of next subject.
 
### Subject two - park at the side of road

    1. perform "where to go"
    2. stop after the steering wheel bottom cross the stop line.
    3. change gear to reverse
    4. keep going backward until the left back mirror botton boarder
       nearly touch the cross dash.    
    5. turn full right   
    6. slow down more, when the thrid vertical dash right
       appear from the veicle body, go stright backward.
    7. when the wheel hit the vertical dash, turn full left.
    8. when the veicle body all goes in the park area,
       stop, keep the angle of wheels.
    9. light up left turnning light
    10. change to gear 1
    11. slowly move forward 
    12. when the right-botton window corner hit the right line
        (45 degree left), go stright forward.
    13. when pass about 3 second
        (2/3 of veicle body out of park area),
        turn one round right.
    14. usal more right turnning needed, adjust by yourself.
    15. perform "where to go"

### Subject three - quadrate turn 

    1. perform "where to go"
    2. before the sign,light up left turnning light.
    3. go slower.
    4. when botton of steering wheel hit the cross dash
       (or you think the front wheels is hitting the middle of road),
       turn full left.
    5. after back wheels pass the turn, perform "where to go"

### Subject four - curve turn

    1. perform "where to go"
    2. match the left back mirror 1/4 lefter position to the left line,
       keep going at same speed.
    3. match the left back wheel a curtain distance(70cm average)
    4. after left turning end, go stright forward.
    5. after 5 second, or move your head left-up a bit
       (hit the veicle silling),
       when the left line frontest point at the door handle position,
       turn right one round.
    6. keep matching the left line frontest point
       at the door handle positon.
    7. after the left line end, go stright forward.
    8. perform "wher to go"

### Subject five - asternway to park    

    1. perform "where to go"
    2. find an empty park area  
    3. close to the right line, keep a  70 cm gap
    4. when the shouder hit the cross dash in park area,
       turn full right
    5. when the rain brush reviet hit the left corner
       of park rectangle, go stright forward.
    6. when the back wheels cross the left line, turn full left.
    7. align rain brush reviet with sign tube
    8. slow down a little bit, stop when the turnning wheel
       botton boarder hit the cross dash.
    9. change gear to reverse.
    10. go backward, when the back mirror button nearly
        (50cm gap) hit the cross dash, turn full right.
    11. look at right back mirror, when viecle body hit
        the thrid vertical dash right boarder,
        go stright backward.
    12. when door handle middle touch the vertical dashes,
        turn full right again.
    13. look at left back mirror, when the veicle body align
        with left line(more a little bit), go stright backward.
    14. adjust the position by experience.
    15. when the center of wheel cross a bit of the back line, stop.
    16. change to gear 1.
    17. go stright forward
    18. when the shoulder hit the cross dash(and line),
        turn left a round and a half
    19. keep goning, able to be faster.
    20. when the front wheel cross the cross dash, stop.
    21. chagne to gear reverse.
    22. go backward, don't turn your steering wheel.
    23. look at left back mirror, when the wheel gona to hit
        the position of left first(in mirror) dash left boarder
        exten line(extent front 50 cm) turn full right
    24. redo step 13
    25. redo step 14
    26. redo step 15
    27. redo step 16
    28. redo step 17  
    29. when the shoulder hit the ross dash(and line),
        turn right a round.
    30. when the back wheel hit the cross dash, turn full right.
    31. stop after front wheel cross the cross dash(another).
    33. finish!
